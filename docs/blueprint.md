# **App Name**: MarketMix Navigator

## Core Features:

- MMM Core Performance Dashboard: Displays Actual vs Predicted Sales (Line Chart), Sales Decomposition (Stacked Area), Channel Contribution Chart (Bar or 100% stacked), and Channel ROAS Comparison (Bar Chart) for core MMM performance insights.
- Price Effect Insights Dashboard: Shows Price Trend Over Time (Line), Price vs Revenue Scatter Plot, Price Elasticity Curve (Demand Curve), Revenue vs Price Curve, Profit vs Price Curve, and Price Ã— Promo Interaction Heatmap.
- Marketing & Promo Impact Dashboard: Provides Promo vs Non-Promo Sales Comparison (Box/Bar), Advertising Saturation Curves (by channel), and Adstock Effect Chart (Media lag impact).
- Competition & Market Forces Dashboard: Presents Competitor Price vs Own Price Trend (Line Chart) and Market Share vs Price Gap (Scatter/Line) to understand external factors.
- KPI Metric Cards: Calculates and displays Total Revenue, Baseline Revenue, Incremental Revenue, Total Marketing Spend, Total ROAS, Price Elasticity, Optimal Prices (Revenue & Profit maximizing), Promo Uplift, and Competitor Price Pressure Index.
- Automated Insights: Leverage a generative AI tool to automatically identify key trends and insights from the dashboard data. This tool will use reasoning to incorporate various charts and metrics in order to produce accurate output.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Indigo (#4B0082) to evoke a sense of sophistication and data-driven insights.
- Background color: Very light gray (#F0F0F5), almost white, to ensure data visualization clarity. 
- Accent color: Muted teal (#008080) to provide a visually distinct and complementary highlight for key data points.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a sans-serif font, for clean and modern readability.
- Use consistent and simple icons to represent different metrics and categories, enhancing visual comprehension.
- Organize each dashboard section in a clear, grid-based layout to facilitate easy navigation and data interpretation.
- Implement subtle animations for transitions and data updates to enhance user engagement without being distracting.
